My First Blog

Posted on 2022-05-27

Wow, finally. It took me about 3 to 4 hours to build this site from scratch. Used Hexo and Github Page to generate this. I have built another site using the same tools during the time I did my graduating project, but I ditched it very soon because I love the new and hate the old LOL.

I will try to express myself more smoothly and naturally. But my fingers and my mouth itched to switch back to JP or CN. I don’t know how to start everything. Anyway, it’s a doubled challenge for me to write a blog AND write things I am not familiar with in English. I have always been a perfectionist, which stops me from completing any non-compulsory work or project, I mean, every time. When I finally decide to pursue a career in this field, I feel a urgent need to establish my knowledge and learning system by recording my learning progress.

let me introduce myself first. I am 0098, I graduated from B*PT last year and my major was MIS. I am not complaining about how terrible undergraduate education in CN has been, but I must say that my undergraduate life was a disaster. I don’t know if it is just me or if everyone does the same, when I graduated, I feel not able to take any job in this world because I don’t have any skill that earns money. But I have been a lucky guy for many years. My parents allowed me to stay home after I failed my graduate entrance exam to a JP research center in BJ. Yes, after so many years studying science, I decided to choose arts.

But I fail the exam mainly because I hate memorizing lines from political books (typical). And I turned to a different education system to apply for graduate school overseas, instead of taking another heart-breaking exam in CN. Too many CN students suffer from it. Last month, I was enrolled in a university in HK, a one-year taught master’s program. Still, MIS related discipline, but this time I am more determined about my future career than ever.

I am planning to complete CS61A and CS61B in three months, before I go to HK, finishing all the lectures, labs, and homework. I am not new to programming, but I am a novice when it comes to computer science. I will record my homework answers and questions here(in English as much as possible), and write some diaries like this. And the Milestone tag is for important events, like my first day in graduate school, which I am excitedly waiting for, and also the first day publishing an all-English post just like today.
